About

“ We're tired of using spreadsheets and papercards for networking ”

We always want to know who we should be talking to at an event. We either try to talk to as many people as we can but that usually ends up being a very limited number of people or we share Excel files or Google spreadsheets to create attendee lists and short summaries for attendees. These spreadsheets are not very professional and anyone can edit other people's information.

Cardstak creates professional cards with only one click (by pulling your Linkedin information) and shares them in a beautiful layout. You can filter by roles and read attendee bios to know beforehand who you should be talking to at an event. You can contact attendees without adding them on Linkedin, paying for In-Mail messages or friending them on Facebook. You can also take notes and save cards for future use.

How It Works

Anyone can create a stak and any stak created has a unique code, and this code is embedded in the URL. For instance, a stak could have the following URL http://cardstak.com/index.php?event_id=9cPOrV4VKx. The last piece 9cPOrV4VKx, is the stak code.

Anyone who you share the URL (with the embedded code) with, will be able to see the stak and the user profiles. Those who do not have the URL will not be able to see the stak. There is no public listing of staks created. To add your card for a specific stak (or to create an stak) you will need to Login.

Sharing options are provided on the left side of each stak. You can use these options to share the URL of the stak with your contacts and social/professional network.
